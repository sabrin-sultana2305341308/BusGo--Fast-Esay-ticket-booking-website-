Ticket Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Rules:

  • Free cancellation within 24 hours of booking (full refund)
  • 25% cancellation fee if cancelled 12–24 hours before departure
  • 50% cancellation fee if cancelled 6–12 hours before departure
  • No refund within 6 hours of departure
  • Refund processing takes 5–7 working days
  • Cancellation must be done through our support email or via the My Tickets section

Note: All cancellations are subject to availability and operator policies.
